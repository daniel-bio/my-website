Projects

I care about how biology and health connect to how people live. These projects show how I think and what I’m working toward—work shaped by curiosity, empathy, and human connection, with the hope of empowering others.

  • Personal Site – I made this site to be honest about who I am and what matters to me.
  • Biotech & Aging Data Project – I'm working with biological data—like single-cell expression, gene annotations, and metabolism—to better understand aging and empower others with better decisions in health and research.
  • Experimental Dashboard – A small project to try out ways of showing lab data more clearly, inspired by my experience at Cytiva.
